About

Welcome to TOMMYCOM. This website is an organized representation of me and things I deem important in my life. It is ever-changing and growing in appearance and content, as am I. The website beyond the Home page is not designed for you, it is designed for me, but you are welcome to explore it.

Objectives

TOMMYCOM has several objectives:

  • An objective of TOMMYCOM is to be a reflection of my life. It documents my hobbies, interests, creative projects, thoughts, etc.

  • An objective of TOMMYCOM is to document and record the media I consume: books, movies, games, articles, etc. Also the media I create: projects, games, essays, etc.

  • An objective of TOMMYCOM is to be updated regularly. This might be large-scale, or small updates to individual parts. It is meant for me to develop and reflect on.

  • An objective of TOMMYCOM is to be a step removed from capitalist corporations. The correct way to view TOMMYCOM is not to. The next best way to view it is with free or open-source software.
