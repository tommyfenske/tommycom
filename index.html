About

This website is an organized representation of me and things I deem important in my life. It is ever-changing and growing in appearance and content, as am I. This website is not made for you, it is made for me, but you are welcome to explore it. It has several objectives:

  • An objective of TOMMYCOM is to be a reflection of my life. It documents my hobbies, interests, creative projects, thoughts, etc.

  • An objective of TOMMYCOM is to document and record the media I consume: books, movies, games, articles, etc. Also the media I create projects, games, essays, etc.

  • An objective of TOMMYCOM is to be updated regularly. This might be large-scale, or small updates to individual parts. It is meant for me to develop and reflect on.

  • An objective of TOMMYCOM is to be a step removed from capitalist corporations. The correct way to view TOMMYCOM is not to. The next best way to view it is with FOSS. It is my pseudo-replacement for other social media.
