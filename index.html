TOMMYCOM

Status: always changing

About

Welcome to TOMMYCOM. This website is an organized representation of me and things I deem important in my life. It is ever-changing and growing in appearance and content, as am I. The website is largely designed for me, not for others, but you are welcome to explore it. TOMMYCOM is not necessarily designed to be user friendly, and it is definitely not designed for mobile. If you are viewing it on a phone and have access to a computer, use that instead. If you are viewing it on a phone and do not have a computer nearby, turn it off and try experiencing the world around you instead.

Objectives

TOMMYCOM has several objectives:

  • An objective of TOMMYCOM is to be a reflection of my life. It documents my hobbies, interests, creative endeavours, and anything else I deem important. TOMMYCOM records the media I consume: books, movies, games, articles, etc. Also the media I create: projects, games, essays, etc.

  • An objective of TOMMYCOM is to be updated over time. This might be large-scale, or small updates to individual parts. It is a long-term project that is meant for me to dedicate time and effort into developing. There is no end product, because it theoretically will never end.

  • An objective of TOMMYCOM is to resist consumerism and, to an extent, capitalism. The correct way to view TOMMYCOM is not to. The next best way to view it is with free or open-source software. Links to capitalist corporations will be limited.
