Catalogs of my various collections. These are not lists of every piece of media I have engaged with, but are lists reflecting the media I own. They are not necessarily in order, but more recently consumed media are more likely to be at the top. Items in these lists are only inlcuded if I:

  1. own a physical copy of the corresponding media, or
  2. have free access to the source code/files of the corresponding media, or
  3. own a DRM-free digital copy that can be accessed without relying on a network connection or corporations for access.
